Mentoring

Schedule a mentor session, or host one!



Mentoring Purpose

The purpose of mentoring in the beginning of the season is to prepare members for Tryouts. Mentoring also may occur during the season, depending on the following invitationals/competitions.


Mentor Expectations

Behavior

Mentors are not to fool around. Members can report their behavior if mentoring sessions aren't used wisely.

Mentors' Responsibility

It is the mentor's responsibility to teach mentees basics of events, but it isn't their duty to help members study.


Mentee Expectations

Note: It is highly recommended for members to come to mentoring-sessions!

Behavior

Mentees are to behave as they are learning in a classroom. Members can report peers' behaviors to us.
