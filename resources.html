Suggestions and Resources

Here are some rules and resources to help yourself get started!

How do you find resources? Check Study Tab!


Sci-Oly Wiki

For learning basic science concepts of certain events, the Sci-Oly wiki has the fundamentals you need. After learning the concepts stated on Sci-Oly-Wiki, you may researching for deeper information.

Gelinas Google Drive

Our Google Drive is a home for practice tests from the past! Utilize these tests (pdf/documents) wisely. Request access if you're a member of our team but do not have access.

Scilympiad

Scilympiad is the website you are required to use for any event you're participating in a virtual competition. Check if you are able to log in prior to competitions with your given username and password! If not, you must contact one of the coaches.

SOINC - Official SCIO Website

This website is the official site of SCIO, and holds general information on the rules of events, future events, news, and more. You can also find Q & A's, so if you're ever stuck, try looking there!

SkyCiv

SkyCiv is a Structural Analysis and Design Software used in a few tech events, and is pretty usefull. There are two planes that you can get a discount on, the scio plan ($50 for 8 month) or the student plan ($14 - $10 per month depending on the plan)
